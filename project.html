Bainbridge Island Rideshare Mobile App

Made With:

The rideshare app is similar to Waze or SmartRideShare and mainly serves residents who need a ride (e.g. to school, to a pub, to ferry) to find other residents that have a ride to give.

  • Built a rideshare mobile application for the residents on Bainbridge Island. The app is like Waze and SmartRideShare, but it helps the residents find a ride through other residents.
  • Consisted of a 4-person team. The app includes sign-up/sign-in capabilities, user authorities, ride requests, ride offers, ride information, user profile information and an edit profile/ride information option.
